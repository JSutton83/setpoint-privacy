Privacy Policy for Set Point

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Overview

Set Point ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Set Point does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data remains on your device.

Information We Access

Match History

  • The app stores your match scores and results locally on your device
  • This includes: match dates, scores, scoring mode used
  • Purpose: To provide you with historical match records
  • Storage: All match data is stored locally on your device only
  • Sharing: Match data never leaves your device

Watch Connectivity

When using the Apple Watch companion app:

  • Match scores sync between your iPhone and Apple Watch via Apple's WatchConnectivity framework
  • This communication happens directly between your devices over Bluetooth/Wi-Fi
  • No data is sent to external servers

Data Usage

We use the data you create in Set Point to:

  • Display current match scores
  • Track match statistics
  • Show your match history

Third-Party Access

Set Point does NOT:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements
  • Require account creation or login
  • Connect to external servers

Your Control

You can:

  • Delete individual matches from your history within the app
  • Delete all app data by deleting the Set Point app from your device

Children's Privacy

Set Point does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date.
